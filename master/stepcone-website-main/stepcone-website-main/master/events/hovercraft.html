HOVERCRAFT RACE

Hovercrafts are remote controlled boat model project. They hover on a cushion of pressurized air. The race is conducted on plane surface. Two boats are released at a time and race held between them winner boat is qualified to next round. The remote control is using with Bluetooth/Wi-fi module.
