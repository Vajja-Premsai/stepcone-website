CHEM-O-ROVER

The chemical boat competition is always good. For this, you need water bottles, baking soda, vinegar, a nail or a drill, and either foam for the boat version or wheels for the car version. You poke a hole in the cap of the water bottle and mix the baking soda and vinegar inside. As they foam up and increase the pressure in the bottle, you'll get a jet out of the hole in the cap and that will push the bottle forward. You typically have to carry a certain weight and try to hit a certain distance of travel to decide a winner.
