SARATHY

Sarathy-the botmaster is basically a hotfoot bot race. The event is aimed to enhance the bot controlling skills of the participants. A special bot is provided to the participants, during the respective time slot allocated. The participant need to showcase their driving skills of the bot along the track provided.The winner and runner of the event will be evaluated based on the time taken to complete path. It is a individual game. Registration fee is 200/- per head. The participant will be allocated with different time slots. During the respective time slot, participant will be given by a special bot. The participant has to take over the bot and need to move the bot across the path provided(special track). Participant need to finish the path as soon as possible.
