ANDROID STAR

Android Star is similar to Hackathon. Here in this, we will give a problem statement to the teams who have registered. They have to come up with a solution and have to build an android app. And after the completion of the time given to them for building their app their team leader have to explain their approach and also have to explain how efficient will their solution will work. They will be assessed by the judges and then the team who scores the highest will be declared as winners.
